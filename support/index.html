FlipStack — Support

LearningFlashcards

Common issues

  • Cards not saving: Ensure you have storage space and try closing/reopening the app.
  • Image add fails: Verify Photos permissions in Settings > Privacy & Security > Photos.
  • Notifications not firing: Confirm alerts allowed in Settings > Notifications > FlipStack.

Contact

Email info@smartstacks.app

LiftStack — Support

StrengthTracking

Common issues

  • Workout not saving: Check for low storage, retry after restarting the app.
  • Units (kg/lb): Adjust in Settings within the app (if available) or your device’s Region settings.

Contact

Email info@smartstacks.app

FastStack — Support

FastingWellness

Common issues

  • Reminder timing off: Ensure Background App Refresh is enabled in Settings.
  • Streaks missing: Confirm device time and timezone are set automatically.

Email info@smartstacks.app

RunStack — Support

RunningC25K

Common issues

  • GPS not tracking: Allow Location access in Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services.
  • Audio cues missing: Check the device mute switch and in-app sound settings.
